Proposed Methodology

The diagram outlines the process of lung disease detection using deep learning. It starts with Data Acquisition, sourcing pneumonia and chest X-ray images. In the Preprocessing stage, tasks like image resizing and normalization are performed to prepare the data. Next, Training Algorithms such as CNNs, VGG16, and VGG19 are applied for feature extraction and learning. The Training phase includes data augmentation and metrics evaluation like accuracy, recall, F1-score, and precision. Finally, the Classifications stage predicts the presence of pneumonia or lung disorders, determining whether a disease is present or not. The workflow ensures efficient and accurate diagnosis.
